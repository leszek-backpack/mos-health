# IC Project — Trigger Map

**Client:** IC Project (icproject.com)
**Market:** Polish SMEs — Construction & Manufacturing
**Purpose:** Map trigger events per vertical with detection methods and response playbooks

---

## Construction Triggers

### 1. New Contract Win / Tender Award

| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Event** | Company wins a public tender or announces a new development project |
| **Detection** | BZP (Biuletyn Zamówień Publicznych), TED database, company press releases, construction industry portals (Budowlany.pl, PropertyNews.pl) |
| **Urgency** | HIGH (7-day window — company is in planning phase, tool decisions happen now) |
| **Message angle** | "Koordynacja nowego projektu [name] to moment, kiedy najczęściej okazuje się, że Excel i email nie wystarczą..." |
| **Target persona** | Director of Projects (champion) |
| **Cadence** | Immediate outreach within 48h of detection |

---

### 2. Multi-site Expansion

| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Event** | Opening projects in new cities/regions (like ATAL expanding beyond Krakow) |
| **Detection** | Company announcements, local building permits, KRS branch registrations |
| **Urgency** | HIGH (14-day window) |
| **Message angle** | "Kiedy ATAL rozszerzał działalność na 7 miast, koordynacja stała się ich największym wyzwaniem — rozwiązali to z IC Project..." |
| **Target persona** | Director of Projects, then CFO |
| **Cadence** | Outreach within 72h, escalate to CFO in step 3 |

---

### 3. Hiring Project Managers / Site Coordinators

| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Event** | Job postings for PM roles, especially multiple at once |
| **Detection** | LinkedIn Jobs, Pracuj.pl, company career pages |
| **Urgency** | MEDIUM (21-day window — hiring cycle is 1-3 months) |
| **Message angle** | "Zatrudnianie nowych kierowników projektów to dobry moment na standaryzację narzędzi — zanim każdy zacznie pracować po swojemu..." |
| **Target persona** | Director of Projects |
| **Cadence** | Outreach within 5 business days |

---

### 4. Leadership Change in Operations

| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Event** | New VP Operations, Director of Projects, or COO appointment |
| **Detection** | LinkedIn, company announcements, press |
| **Urgency** | MEDIUM (30-day window — new leaders make tool changes in first 90 days) |
| **Message angle** | New leader brings fresh perspective, tool evaluation is natural. "Nowi dyrektorzy operacyjni najczęściej zaczynają od przeglądu narzędzi — chętnie pokażę, co zmieniło się w zarządzaniu projektami budowlanymi..." |
| **Target persona** | The new leader directly |
| **Cadence** | Outreach 2-3 weeks after appointment (let them settle in) |

---

### 5. Competitor Tool Contract Expiry / Churn

| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Event** | Signals of dissatisfaction with current tools (LinkedIn complaints, review site signals, job posting mentioning "tool evaluation") |
| **Detection** | G2/Capterra reviews, LinkedIn posts, industry forums, job postings with "wdrożenie nowego systemu" language |
| **Urgency** | HIGHEST (3-day window — actively evaluating alternatives) |
| **Message angle** | Direct comparison positioning. "Widziałem, że szukacie alternatywy dla [competitor] — firmy budowlane, które przeszły na IC Project, najczęściej wskazują na [specific advantage]..." |
| **Target persona** | Whoever posted/signaled the dissatisfaction |
| **Cadence** | Same-day outreach, phone call within 24h |

---

### 6. Construction Market Regulatory Changes

| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Event** | New building regulations, BIM mandates, reporting requirements |
| **Detection** | Government announcements, industry associations (PZITB, PIG), Dziennik Ustaw |
| **Urgency** | LOW (60-day window — regulatory changes have long implementation timelines) |
| **Message angle** | "Nowe wymogi [regulation] oznaczają więcej dokumentacji i raportowania — automatyzacja tego procesu to kwestia przetrwania, nie wygody..." |
| **Target persona** | Director of Projects, CFO |
| **Cadence** | Outreach within 2 weeks of announcement, content-led approach |

---

### 7. Industry Event / Conference

| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Event** | Company presenting or attending BUDMA, 4 Design Days, Real Estate conferences |
| **Detection** | Event attendee lists, LinkedIn posts, company news, event sponsor lists |
| **Urgency** | MEDIUM (7-day window around event — 3 days before to 4 days after) |
| **Message angle** | Reference the event, share industry insight. "Widziałem, że będziecie na BUDMA — chętnie pokażę, jak firmy budowlane Waszej skali rozwiązują koordynację multi-site..." |
| **Target persona** | Attendee (usually Director-level) |
| **Cadence** | Pre-event outreach 3 days before, follow-up day after event |

---

### 8. Financial Results / Annual Report

| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Event** | Published financial results showing growth, new investments planned |
| **Detection** | KRS filings, GPW (stock exchange) reports, company announcements, Monitor Polski B |
| **Urgency** | LOW (30-day window) |
| **Message angle** | Growth = more projects = more coordination needs. "Wzrost przychodów {{company_name}} o [X]% to imponujący wynik — z doświadczenia wiemy, że firmy na tym etapie potrzebują lepszych narzędzi do koordynacji rosnącej liczby projektów..." |
| **Target persona** | CFO |
| **Cadence** | Outreach within 2 weeks of publication |

## Trigger Response Playbook

### HIGHEST Urgency (3-day response window)

**Triggers:** Competitor tool churn signals

| Parameter | Requirement |
|---|---|
| **Response time SLA** | Same day — outreach within 4 business hours of detection |
| **Outreach channel** | LinkedIn DM (immediate) + Email (same day) + Phone call (within 24h) |
| **Personalization minimum** | Score 4 (Tier 2 variables + specific signal reference) |
| **Follow-up cadence** | Day 0: LinkedIn DM + Email. Day 1: Phone call. Day 3: Email follow-up with comparison content. Day 7: Final attempt via different channel. |
| **Escalation** | If no response by Day 3, multi-thread to second persona at same company |
| **Content assets** | Competitor comparison sheet, migration guide, ROI calculator |

---

### HIGH Urgency (7-14 day response window)

**Triggers:** New contract win, multi-site expansion, plant expansion, new product launch, M&A activity

| Parameter | Requirement |
|---|---|
| **Response time SLA** | Within 48 hours of detection |
| **Outreach channel** | Email (primary) + LinkedIn connection request with note (same day) |
| **Personalization minimum** | Score 4 (Tier 2 variables + Bucket A/B/C signal) |
| **Follow-up cadence** | Day 0: Email + LinkedIn. Day 3: Email follow-up with relevant case study. Day 7: LinkedIn message (if connected) or call. Day 14: Break-up email with valuable content. |
| **Escalation** | If no response by Day 7, multi-thread to second persona (CFO or CEO) |
| **Content assets** | Relevant case study (ATAL for construction, Wawel for manufacturing), industry benchmark report |

---

### MEDIUM Urgency (7-30 day response window)

**Triggers:** Hiring signals, leadership changes, industry events, quality certifications

| Parameter | Requirement |
|---|---|
| **Response time SLA** | Within 5 business days of detection |
| **Outreach channel** | Email (primary), LinkedIn connection request (secondary) |
| **Personalization minimum** | Score 3 (Tier 1-2 variables + signal reference) |
| **Follow-up cadence** | Day 0: Email. Day 5: Email follow-up. Day 12: LinkedIn message or value-add content. Day 21: Final email with different angle. Day 30: Move to nurture sequence. |
| **Escalation** | No multi-threading unless Tier 1 account |
| **Content assets** | Industry guide, checklist, or blog post relevant to the trigger |

---

### LOW Urgency (30-60 day response window)

**Triggers:** Regulatory changes, financial results/growth signals

| Parameter | Requirement |
|---|---|
| **Response time SLA** | Within 2 weeks of detection |
| **Outreach channel** | Email only (content-led approach) |
| **Personalization minimum** | Score 2 acceptable (Tier 1 variables + industry context) |
| **Follow-up cadence** | Day 0: Content-led email (guide, report, or insight). Day 10: Follow-up referencing content. Day 25: Different angle email. Day 45: Move to nurture sequence. |
| **Escalation** | None — low urgency signals are best used to warm accounts for future higher-urgency triggers |
| **Content assets** | Industry trend report, regulatory compliance guide, ROI benchmark study |

## Detection Infrastructure Summary

### Automated Monitoring (set up once, runs continuously)

| Source | Tool | Frequency | Triggers Covered |
|---|---|---|---|
| BZP / TED | RSS feed + keyword alerts | Daily | Contract wins |
| LinkedIn Jobs | Sales Navigator alerts / Clay | Daily | Hiring signals |
| Google Alerts | Google Alerts (company names) | Real-time | Press, expansion, M&A, launches |
| KRS / CEIDG | API monitoring or manual check | Weekly | Leadership changes, new branches, M&A |
| G2 / Capterra | Review monitoring | Weekly | Competitor churn signals |
| Industry portals | RSS feeds (Budowlany.pl, WNP.pl, Produkcja.expert) | Daily | All construction/manufacturing triggers |

### Event-based Monitoring (periodic, calendar-driven)

| Source | Tool | Frequency | Triggers Covered |
|---|---|---|---|
| Trade fairs | Manual calendar tracking | Monthly review | BUDMA, Polagra, ITM, etc. |
| Financial reports | KRS / GPW filings calendar | Quarterly | Financial growth signals |
| Certification bodies | Manual check | Quarterly | Quality certification pursuit |

### Priority Order for Setup
1. **Week 1:** Google Alerts for all Tier 1 accounts + BZP/TED RSS feeds
2. **Week 2:** LinkedIn Jobs monitoring via Sales Navigator + Clay enrichment pipeline
3. **Week 3:** KRS monitoring for leadership/M&A + industry portal RSS feeds
4. **Week 4:** G2/Capterra monitoring + trade fair calendar setup
