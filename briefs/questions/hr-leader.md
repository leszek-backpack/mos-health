# Discovery Questions — HR / People Ops Leader

**Goal:** Understand strategic priorities, budget authority, and benefits effectiveness

## Opening Context Questions (2-3 min)

1. **What are your top 3 people priorities for this year?**
   - *Why ask:* Reveals if wellness/benefits align with their strategic goals

2. **What numbers or outcomes matter most for your role?** *OR* **How is your team measured on success?**
   - *Why ask:* Uncovers if they track engagement, retention, or productivity metrics

## Problem/Needs Discovery (5-7 min)

3. **What's working well with your current benefits package, and where do you see gaps?** *OR* **Walk me through your current benefits stack. For each one, how many people are *actually* using it?**
   - *Why ask:* Gets both strengths and pain points in one question; utilization data reveals effectiveness

4. **How do employees currently engage with health and wellness benefits?**
   - *Why ask:* Usage rates, satisfaction, and participation barriers

5. **What feedback do you hear most often from employees about their health or energy levels at work?**
   - *Why ask:* Reveals unmet needs and pain points directly from employee voice

6. **How do you measure success of benefits? Are you tracking metrics like absenteeism reduction, productivity increases, or employee satisfaction?**
   - *Why ask:* Understand their data maturity and what ROI metrics matter

## Purchase Process (4-6 min)

7. **How do you typically evaluate and approve new benefits? Who's involved, what's the timeline, and what criteria matter most?**
   - *Why ask:* Gets decision process, stakeholders, and approval structure in one question

8. **What's your current monthly spend per employee on wellness benefits?** *OR* **Can you walk me through your total wellness budget? How is it broken down?**
   - *Why ask:* Budget qualification — understand total spend and allocation

9. **When you've added benefits in the past, what made you choose one vendor over another?**
   - *Why ask:* Decision criteria and competitive insights — what wins deals

## Objection Handling (2-3 min)

10. **What would make you hesitate to add a new benefit right now?**
    - *Why ask:* Proactively surfaces concerns about budget, culture fit, effectiveness, or timing
